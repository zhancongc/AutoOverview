# Product Overview for Paddle Merchant Application

## Product Name

AutoOverview — AI-Powered Academic Literature Review Generator

## Product Description

AutoOverview is a SaaS platform that helps university students and academic researchers generate comprehensive literature reviews in minutes. Users input a research topic, and the system automatically searches authoritative academic databases (Semantic Scholar, CrossRef, IEEE Xplore, Web of Science), selects relevant peer-reviewed papers, and produces a structured literature review with 100% real citations — no hallucinated references.

## Target Market

University students, graduate researchers, and academics in the United States, Canada, United Kingdom, and European Union who need to write literature reviews as part of their coursework, thesis, or research papers.

## How It Works

1. User enters a research topic (e.g., "Transformer models in medical imaging")
2. The system searches academic databases and retrieves 50+ relevant papers
3. An AI engine analyzes, categorizes, and synthesizes the literature
4. A formatted review is generated in ~5 minutes with proper academic citations

## Key Features

- **Real Citations Only** — All references are verified against actual academic databases, eliminating hallucinated sources common in generic AI tools
- **200M+ Paper Database** — Access to major academic repositories including Semantic Scholar, CrossRef, IEEE Xplore, and Web of Science
- **IEEE Format** — Output formatted to standard academic citation styles
- **PDF & Word Export** — Download reviews for direct use in research papers
- **Bilingual** — Available in English and Chinese
- **Academic Integrity** — Designed as a research assistant tool, not a paper-writing service
