# Paddle 支付接入申请攻略

> 2026-04-19 更新

## 背景

Paddle 的 AUP (Acceptable Use Policy) 明确禁止 "essay and paper mills, ghostwriting services"（Category 12）。上次申请因"AI 生成文献综述"被判定为学术不端渠道而被拒。

## 核心策略

**不要说**："AI generates literature reviews for you"

**应该说**："Academic literature discovery and organization platform"

对标产品：Elicit、Connected Papers、Consensus、Semantic Scholar — 都是帮研究者找论文、整理文献的工具。

## 2. Product Description

Danmo Scholar is an academic literature discovery and organization platform that helps researchers, graduate students, and scholars efficiently find, compare, and organize relevant academic papers.

Our platform provides three core capabilities:

**Literature Search & Discovery** — Search across millions of academic papers from OpenAlex, one of the world's largest open academic databases. Find relevant research quickly with AI-assisted query optimization.

**Paper Comparison Matrix** — Compare methodologies, findings, and key metrics across multiple papers side-by-side. Export comparisons in Markdown or Word format for your research workflow.

**Literature Organization** — Automatically organize discovered papers with proper citation formatting (IEEE, APA, MLA, GB/T 7714). Export references in BibTeX, RIS, or Word formats for seamless integration with reference managers like Zotero, Mendeley, and EndNote.

**Designed for legitimate academic research**, Danmo Scholar is a discovery and organization tool — similar to Connected Papers, Elicit, and Semantic Scholar. All results include verification links to original sources via Google Scholar and DOI.
